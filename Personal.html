One Piece

Reasons Why One Piece Is My Favorite Anime

  1. The show provides a unique story line that falls into government corruption and hard hitting topics(slavery,racism,superiority complex,etc)
  2. Tragic pasts are evident within the straw hat pirates enabling them the reasoning to become pirates
  3. Massive growth is shown in character development throughout the episodes which can be one of the reasons the shows longevity is existent
  4. One piece itself is a emotional rollercoster, that can either leave a grown man to tears or die from comical relief
  5. Since the show is a very long watch (on going for 20 years)you get a sense of connection with the characters as they triumph through adversity
